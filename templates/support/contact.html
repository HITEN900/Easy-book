Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a bus ticket?
Simply search for your route using the home page search bar, select your preferred bus, choose seats, enter passenger details, and complete payment. Your ticket will be instantly confirmed!
How can I cancel my booking?
You can cancel your booking from the "My Bookings" section. Go to your dashboard, find the booking, and click the cancel button. Refunds will be processed as per the cancellation policy.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking from all major banks, and digital wallets.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are automatically processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days after cancellation. You'll receive a confirmation email once processed.
Can I change my booking date?
Date changes depend on the bus operator's policy. Please contact our support team with your booking reference for assistance with date changes.
What documents do I need for boarding?
You need to carry a valid government ID (Aadhar, Passport, Driving License) and a printout or digital copy of your ticket. The name on ID should match the passenger details.
